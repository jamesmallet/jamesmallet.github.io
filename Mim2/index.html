History of UCL Biology

It is intended to provide links here to items of historical interest about the UCL Biology Department and forerunner departments (Botany, Zoology, Microbiology, The Galton Laboratory, etc.). We start with information about the History of the Galton Laboratory.

Prof. Cedric A.B. Smith in 1995 gave a seminar in the Galton Lab entitled "The Evolution of the Galton Laboratory" (click to hear the recording in MP3 format). This contains information about statisticians and geneticists Karl and Egon Pearson, R.A. Fisher, J.B.S. Haldane and many others whom Smith knew.

See also the Galton Laboratory, the Galton Collection and www.galton.org
