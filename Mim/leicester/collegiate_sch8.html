Collegiate School, Leicester.  These buildings, once perhaps a residential block, now house the Leicester City Council Education Department's Psychology Service.  "The service provides specialist support and assistance to schools, works closely in partnership with parents, teachers, children and others organisations providing services to children/young people (aged 0-19 years) within the local Authority and Health Service."
