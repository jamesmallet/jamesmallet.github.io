Main buildings of the Collegiate School, Leicester.  This building has been renamed "The Rowans", and forms part of Leicester City Council's Education Services.  The building now houses the Nia Day Centre, which "provides day care for African Caribbean older people living in the community who have mental health difficulties. The focus of the centre is provision of a culturally stimulating environment for older African Caribbean people. The centre aims to provide respite care and support for carers. All service users are encouraged to participate in activities. These include needlecraft, leather, craft, candle-making and gentle relaxation."
