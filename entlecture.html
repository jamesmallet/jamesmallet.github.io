FREDERICK W EDWARDS LECTURE IN ENTOMOLOGY

THE NATURAL HISTORY MUSEUM
FLETT THEATRE
3 May 2000

"Costa Rican caterpillars and their parasitoids:
very host specific"
by
Dr Dan Janzen

Dr Dan Janzen from University of Pennsylvania will give the second FW Edwards annual Lecture in Entomology.  The talk will begin at 14:30 on Wednesday 3 May 2000 in the Flett Theatre, The Natural History Museum, South Kensington.

Entrance is by free tickets, which are of limited number.  They can be obtained in advance from Mrs Pat Marsh (pgm@nhm.ac.uk) giving your name and full postal address.  The ticket has full details of the exact venue.  For further information, see the Dan Janzen/Winnie Hallwachs caterpillar website or contact Linda Pitkin (l.pitkin@nhm.ac.uk).


Organized by the Department of Entomology, The Natural History Museum, in collaboration with the Royal Entomological Society.
